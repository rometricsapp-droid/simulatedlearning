App Purpose & Goal

Master your clinical skills.

ROMetrics is a dedicated training tool for students and early-career learners to refine biomechanical analysis. Signing in allows you to securely track personal progress in Goniometry and MMT simulations to identify areas for improvement.

The Unified Workspace

Comprehensive Training Modules

GT

Goniometry Trainer

How it is used

  • Select model and body position (standing, supine, etc.)
  • Place fulcrum, stationary, and moving arms
  • Track joint action angles during real-time movement

Guided instructional learning for measurement techniques.

MT

Manual Muscle Testing

How it is used

  • Review test positions and movement directions
  • Apply interactive resistance using precision controls
  • Observe hold time and fatigue to assign grades (0-5)

Focuses on understanding grading logic and mechanics.

ML

Motion Lab

How it is used

  • Move joints manually or through predefined actions
  • Observe segment coordination and sequencing
  • Adjust posture and model orientation

Exploratory environment for foundational motion study.

SE

Simulated Evaluations

How they work

  • Analyze case context, clinical clues, and patient history.
  • Make clinical decisions independently without the use of guided prompts.
  • Practice identifying Range of Motion (ROM) and strength deficits.
  • Includes an AI grading engine that objectively analyzes your clinical documentation by comparing your recorded measurements against simulated "gold standards" to provide immediate feedback on your diagnostic accuracy.

Application-based practice for exam preparation.

FL

Forensic Motion Lab

What it focuses on

  • Balance, stability, and compensatory strategies
  • Center of mass and base of support
  • Mechanical reasoning for task outcomes

Answers “why did this happen?” through physics.
